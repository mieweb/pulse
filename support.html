Getting Started with Pulse for Institutional Knowledge Sharing

Welcome to Pulse - the secure institutional knowledge sharing platform! Here's how to create professional training and documentation videos for your organization:

Institutional Use Cases

  • Training Documentation: Create step-by-step training videos for new employees and procedures
  • Process Documentation: Record institutional workflows and standard operating procedures
  • Knowledge Transfer: Capture expert knowledge before staff transitions or retirements
  • Compliance Training: Develop engaging compliance and safety training materials
  • Institutional Memory: Preserve organizational knowledge and best practices
  • Remote Training: Create accessible training content for distributed teams

Basic Recording Features

  • Tap to Record: Quick tap for short clips (perfect for step-by-step instructions)
  • Hold to Record: Hold the button for longer recordings (up to 3 minutes)
  • Drag to Zoom: While holding record, drag up/down to zoom in/out smoothly
  • Pinch to Zoom: Use pinch gestures to zoom the camera before recording
  • Camera Controls: Access flash, camera flip, and video stabilization options

Advanced Segment Management

  • Visual Progress Bar: See all your recorded segments with duration indicators
  • Undo/Redo System: Full undo/redo functionality with persistent storage
  • Smart Reordering: Drag & drop interface with visual drop indicators
  • Draft System: Auto-save your work and continue later
  • Video Library Import: Add existing videos from your photo library

Duration & Time Management

  • Flexible Duration: Set limits from 15 seconds to 3 minutes
  • Auto-Save Preferences: Your duration settings are automatically saved
  • Smart Validation: Prevents setting duration lower than current segments
  • Auto-Stop Recording: Automatically stops at your set limit

Professional Features

  • Hardware Acceleration: Native video processing for optimal performance
  • Cross-Platform: Works seamlessly on iOS and Android
  • Offline Capability: Record without internet connection
  • Secure Storage: All content stored locally on your device

Institutional Best Practices

  • Content Planning: Plan your segments before recording to ensure comprehensive coverage
  • Consistent Quality: Use good lighting and stable positioning for professional results
  • Clear Communication: Speak clearly and at an appropriate pace for your audience
  • Structured Content: Use the segment system to break complex topics into digestible parts
  • Regular Updates: Keep institutional content current and relevant
  • Accessibility: Consider closed captions and clear visual demonstrations

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reorder my video segments?
Record 2 or more segments, then tap the reorder button (reorder icon) in the camera controls. Use the intuitive drag & drop interface with visual drop indicators to rearrange your segments.
Can I import videos from my photo library?
Yes! Tap the "+" button in the camera interface to import videos from your photo library. They'll be added as segments that respect your duration limits and can be reordered with your recorded clips.
How do I set a recording duration limit?
Use the time selector button (clock icon) to choose from 15 seconds to 3 minutes. The app will automatically stop recording at your limit and prevent you from exceeding it.
What happens if I try to set a duration lower than my current segments?
The app will show a validation message asking you to undo some segments first. This prevents data loss and ensures your content stays within the new duration limit.
How does the draft system work?
Your recordings are automatically saved as drafts. You can close the app and return later to continue recording. The app will auto-load your most recent draft when you return.
Can I use the undo/redo feature?
Yes! The undo/redo system is fully functional with persistent storage. You can undo segments and redo them even after closing and reopening the app.
How do I access camera controls?
Tap the camera controls button to access flash, camera flip, and video stabilization options. These controls are available when not recording and help you get the best video quality.
Is Pulse suitable for institutional use?
Absolutely! Pulse is designed specifically for institutional knowledge sharing, training documentation, and professional content creation. All data stays on your device for security.
How can I use Pulse for training documentation?
Pulse is perfect for creating step-by-step training videos. Use segmented recording to break complex procedures into manageable parts, then reorder segments for optimal learning flow.
Is my institutional content secure?
Yes! All your content is stored locally on your device. No data is uploaded to external servers unless you explicitly choose to share it through secure channels.
Can multiple team members use Pulse?
Yes! Pulse works on both iOS and Android devices, making it easy for your entire team to create and share institutional knowledge content.
How do I share completed training videos?
Use your device's native sharing options from the preview screen to share completed videos through your organization's preferred channels (email, cloud storage, etc.).

Troubleshooting Guide

Camera & Recording Issues

  • Camera not working: Check camera permissions in Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera
  • Poor video quality: Ensure good lighting, clean camera lens, and stable device
  • Audio issues: Check microphone permissions in Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone
  • Recording stops unexpectedly: Check available storage space and battery level
  • Zoom not working: Ensure you're using pinch gestures or drag while holding record

Performance & Storage

  • App running slowly: Close other apps, restart Pulse, or restart your device
  • Storage issues: Free up space by deleting unused apps, photos, or old drafts
  • Video processing slow: This is normal for longer videos; the app uses hardware acceleration
  • Memory issues: Avoid recording very long segments; break content into smaller clips

Feature-Specific Issues

  • Reorder not working: Ensure you have at least 2 segments recorded
  • Duration limit not working: Check that you've selected a duration using the time selector
  • Import not working: Grant photo library permissions in Settings > Privacy & Security > Photos
  • Draft not loading: Check if you have any saved drafts in the drafts screen
  • Undo/Redo not working: These features require at least one recorded segment

Institutional Use

  • Content not saving: Ensure you have sufficient device storage for institutional content
  • Sharing issues: Use your device's native sharing options from the preview screen
  • Backup concerns: All content is stored locally; consider regular device backups
  • Team collaboration: Share completed videos through your organization's preferred channels
