Getting Started

Welcome to Pulse! Here's how to get the most out of your video recording experience:

Basic Recording

  • Tap to Record: Tap the record button for quick clips
  • Hold to Record: Hold the button for longer recordings
  • Drag to Zoom: While holding, drag up/down to zoom in/out
  • Pinch to Zoom: Use pinch gestures to zoom the camera

Segment Management

  • View Segments: See all your recorded segments in the progress bar
  • Undo/Redo: Use the undo/redo buttons to manage your segments
  • Reorder: Tap the reorder button (when you have 2+ segments) to rearrange
  • Drag & Drop: In reorder mode, drag segments to new positions

Duration Settings

  • Set Duration: Use the time selector to set recording limits (15s to 3min)
  • Auto-Save: Your duration preference is automatically saved
  • Validation: App prevents setting duration lower than current segments

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reorder my video segments?
Record 2 or more segments, then tap the reorder button (6-dot icon) in the camera controls. Drag any segment to a new position and watch the red line show where it will be placed.
Can I import videos from my photo library?
Yes! Tap the "+" button in the camera interface to import videos from your photo library. They'll be added as segments that you can reorder with your recorded clips.
How do I set a recording duration limit?
Use the time selector button (clock icon) to choose from 15 seconds to 3 minutes. The app will prevent you from recording beyond this limit.
Where are my videos saved?
All videos are saved locally on your device. You can access them through the preview screen and share them using your device's sharing options.
Can I continue recording later?
Yes! Your recordings are automatically saved as drafts. You can close the app and return later to continue recording more segments.
How do I use camera controls?
Tap the camera controls button to access flash, camera flip, and video stabilization options. These controls are only available when not recording.

Troubleshooting

Camera Issues

  • Camera not working: Check that you've granted camera permissions in Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera
  • Poor video quality: Ensure good lighting and clean camera lens
  • Audio issues: Check microphone permissions in Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone

Performance Issues

  • App running slowly: Close other apps and restart Pulse
  • Storage issues: Free up space on your device by deleting unused apps or photos
  • Recording stops unexpectedly: Check available storage space and battery level

Feature Issues

  • Reorder not working: Make sure you have at least 2 segments recorded
  • Duration limit not working: Check that you've selected a duration using the time selector
  • Import not working: Grant photo library permissions in Settings > Privacy & Security > Photos
