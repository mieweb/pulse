# Pulse Vault User Study — Surveys

Three surveys for the IRB-approved study **Adoption and Effectiveness of Pulse Vault for Institutional Knowledge Sharing**. Use these to build forms in Google Forms, Qualtrics, or another tool.

## Files

| File | When | Est. time |
|------|------|-----------|
| `01_Baseline_Survey.md` | After consent, before or right after first use | 3–5 min |
| `02_Midpoint_Survey.md` | ~2 weeks into study | 5–8 min |
| `03_End_of_Study_Survey.md` | ~1 month (or when study closes) | 8–12 min |

## Building the surveys (e.g., Google Forms)

1. **Create three separate forms** (Baseline, Midpoint, End of study) so you can send the right link at the right time.
2. **Copy each question** from the `.md` files. The text in **bold** is the question; the line under *Response type* tells you how to set the question type (single choice, multiple choice, linear scale 1–5, short/long answer).
3. **SUS (System Usability Scale):** Use a **linear scale 1–5** with labels “Strongly disagree” (1) and “Strongly agree” (5). Odd-numbered SUS items (e.g. Q5, Q7, Q9) are positive; even-numbered (Q6, Q8, Q10) are negative—the scale is the same (1–5), scoring is done later (see below).
4. **Optional questions:** Mark clearly as optional so participants know they can skip.
5. **Study period in Q31–Q32:** If your study is exactly one month, use “past month.” Otherwise replace with “study period” or the actual length (e.g. “past 4 weeks”).

## SUS scoring (for your analysis)

- **Odd items** (e.g. Q5, Q7, Q9, Q11, Q13): contribution = (response − 1).
- **Even items** (e.g. Q6, Q8, Q10, Q12, Q14): contribution = (5 − response).
- **SUS score** = sum of all 10 contributions × 2.5 → score from 0 to 100.

Use the same formula for the end-of-study SUS (Q17–Q26).

## IRB / PERA

- Your protocol already describes these surveys (baseline: role, prior video use; midpoint: SUS + satisfaction; end: SUS, satisfaction, video vs. text, barriers).
- If Purdue asks for “surveys” or “instruments,” upload these three `.md` files (or PDFs exported from your form tool) to the **Other Attachments** or section indicated in the SmartForm (see protocol 3.4).

## Linking to usage (optional)

Q4 (Baseline) and Q36 (End) ask for an optional email to link responses to Pulse Vault usage. Only use this if your consent form allows linking survey data to account/usage data, and store responses securely.

## Alignment with protocol

- **Baseline:** Demographics/role, prior use of video for documentation, optional preference and optional email link.
- **Midpoint:** SUS (10 items), satisfaction, optional open-ended feedback.
- **End:** SUS, satisfaction, video vs. text preference and perceived helpfulness, relative use (video vs. text), creation counts, adoption barriers/enablers, optional feedback and optional email link.

These match the study design in your HRP-503c protocol and support your research questions (adoption, usability, video vs. text).
