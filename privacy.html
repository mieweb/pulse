Privacy Policy

Institutional Data Security & Privacy Protection

Last Updated: October 12, 2025

Key Point: Pulse is designed for institutional knowledge sharing. While we collect some data for app functionality, all your videos and content remain secure and private on your device.

Information We Collect

Pulse collects and processes the following information to provide institutional knowledge sharing functionality:

User Content

  • Photos or Videos: Your recorded videos and imported content for institutional knowledge sharing
  • Audio Data: Voice recordings and audio content captured with your videos
  • Product Interaction: How you interact with the app to improve functionality

Identifiers

  • User ID: For authentication and access control in institutional settings
  • Device ID: For app functionality and backend integration (when implemented)

Device Permissions

  • Camera Access: Required for video recording functionality
  • Microphone Access: Required for audio recording with videos
  • Photo Library Access: Required for importing existing videos from your device
  • Device Storage: Required for saving your recorded videos locally

Local Data Storage

  • Video Files: Your recorded videos are stored locally on your device
  • Draft Information: Recording segments and settings are saved locally
  • App Preferences: Your duration settings and app configuration
  • Undo/Redo Data: Segment history for editing functionality

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for app functionality and analytics to improve the institutional knowledge sharing experience:

App Functionality

  • Camera Access: To enable video recording through your device's camera
  • Microphone Access: To record audio along with your videos
  • Photo Library Access: To allow you to import existing videos from your device
  • Device Storage: To save your recordings and app data locally
  • User ID & Device ID: For authentication and access control in institutional settings

Analytics

  • Product Interaction: To understand how users interact with features and improve the app
  • Usage Patterns: To optimize the institutional knowledge sharing workflow
  • Feature Effectiveness: To measure and improve app functionality

Data Storage and Security

Current Storage: All your videos and data are currently stored exclusively on your device. We do not upload, store, or access your personal videos on our servers.

Future Backend Integration: We plan to implement secure backend services for institutional knowledge sharing. When this feature is available, you will have full control over what content is shared and with whom.

Data Encryption: Your local data is protected by your device's built-in security features. Any future backend integration will use industry-standard encryption.

Third-Party Services

Pulse does not currently share your data with any third-party services. We do not use advertising networks or external data processing services. Any future backend integration will be designed specifically for institutional use with appropriate security measures.

Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data and institutional content:

  • Delete Data: You can delete the app at any time to remove all stored data
  • Manage Permissions: You can revoke camera, microphone, or photo library access in your device settings
  • Export Videos: You can export your videos using your device's sharing features
  • Institutional Control: When backend features are available, you'll control what content is shared within your organization
  • Data Portability: All your content remains accessible and can be exported at any time

Institutional Use & Compliance

Pulse is designed for institutional knowledge sharing and training. Organizations using Pulse should:

  • Establish Policies: Create clear policies for video content creation and sharing
  • Data Governance: Ensure compliance with institutional data policies and regulations
  • Access Control: Manage who can create and access institutional content
  • Backup Strategy: Implement appropriate backup procedures for important content
  • Compliance Monitoring: Regular audits of content creation and sharing practices
  • Training Programs: Educate staff on proper use of institutional knowledge sharing tools

Regulatory Compliance

Pulse is designed to support compliance with various institutional and regulatory requirements:

  • FERPA Compliance: Educational institutions can use Pulse while maintaining student privacy
  • HIPAA Considerations: Healthcare institutions should ensure no PHI is recorded in training videos
  • SOX Compliance: Financial institutions can use Pulse for training while maintaining audit trails
  • GDPR Compliance: European institutions can use Pulse with full data control and portability
  • Institutional Policies: Customizable to meet specific organizational data handling requirements

Children's Privacy

Pulse is suitable for users of all ages in institutional settings. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally on the device, parents and institutions have full control over their children's data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically.
