Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last Updated: December 2024

Key Point: Pulse stores all your videos locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or access your personal videos on our servers.

Information We Collect

Pulse collects and processes the following information to provide video recording functionality:

Device Permissions

  • Camera Access: Required for video recording functionality
  • Microphone Access: Required for audio recording with videos
  • Photo Library Access: Required for importing existing videos from your device
  • Device Storage: Required for saving your recorded videos locally

Local Data Storage

  • Video Files: Your recorded videos are stored locally on your device
  • Draft Information: Recording segments and settings are saved locally
  • App Preferences: Your duration settings and app configuration

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to provide the video recording functionality:

  • Camera Access: To enable video recording through your device's camera
  • Microphone Access: To record audio along with your videos
  • Photo Library Access: To allow you to import existing videos from your device
  • Device Storage: To save your recordings and app data locally

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only: All your videos and data are stored exclusively on your device. We do not upload, store, or access your personal videos on our servers.

No Cloud Storage: Pulse does not use any cloud storage services. Your videos remain private and secure on your device.

Data Encryption: Your local data is protected by your device's built-in security features.

Third-Party Services

Pulse does not share your data with any third-party services. We do not use analytics, advertising networks, or any external data processing services.

Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete Data: You can delete the app at any time to remove all stored data
  • Manage Permissions: You can revoke camera, microphone, or photo library access in your device settings
  • Export Videos: You can export your videos using your device's sharing features
  • No Account Required: Pulse doesn't require any user accounts or personal information

Children's Privacy

Pulse is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally on the device, parents have full control over their children's data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically.
